# Restaurant Onboarding — Getting Started

## ZenTable Pe Apna Restaurant Kaise Add Hoga?

Abhi ZenTable ka onboarding **fully managed** hai — restaurant owner ko kuch setup nahi karna padta. ZenTable ki team poora setup karti hai aur ready hone ke baad owner ko handover karti hai.

---

## Onboarding Process

### Step 1 — Contact Karo
- ZenTable se contact karo: `contact@zentable.in` ya website pe
- Apne restaurant ki basic info share karo:
  - Restaurant ka naam aur address
  - Cuisine type
  - Kitni tables hain
  - Kya kya features chahiye (ordering, analytics, AR menu?)

### Step 2 — Demo
- ZenTable team free demo dikhayegi
- Platform ka live walkthrough milega
- Sawaal pooch sakte hain

### Step 3 — Trial Start
- 1 mahine ka free trial activate hoga
- Trial mein milega:
  - Digital menu setup
  - QR codes
  - 5 pre-existing 3D models (agar AR plan liya)
  - Staff accounts
  - Full platform access

### Step 4 — ZenTable Team Setup Karti Hai
Team yeh sab karti hai setup mein:
- Restaurant profile create karna (naam, logo, address, timings, social links)
- Menu items add karna (categories, images, prices, descriptions)
- Tables configure karna
- QR codes generate karna
- AR models link karna (agar applicable)
- Staff accounts banana (owner + initial staff)

### Step 5 — Handover & Training
- ZenTable team live walkthrough deti hai
- Owner ko dashboard explain kiya jaata hai
- Staff ko unka dashboard explain kiya jaata hai
- Go-live!

---

## Owner Ko Kya Prepare Karna Chahiye?
Onboarding smooth karne ke liye yeh ready rakho:

- **Menu list** — items, categories, prices
- **Food photos** — menu images ke liye (high quality preferred)
- **Restaurant logo** — PNG/JPG format
- **Banner image** — homepage ke liye
- **Basic info** — address, phone, timings, social media links
- **Staff list** — naam aur role (waiter/kitchen/counter)

---

## Timeline
Typically 3-5 business days mein restaurant live ho jaata hai setup ke baad. AR menu ke saath thoda zyada time lag sakta hai (3D model creation).
