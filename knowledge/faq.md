# FAQ — Frequently Asked Questions

## General

**Q: ZenTable kya hai?**
A: ZenTable ek restaurant management platform hai. Digital menu, AR menu, table ordering, kitchen management, billing, aur analytics — sab ek jagah. Restaurants apna operation modern aur efficient bana sakte hain bina kisi expensive hardware ke.

**Q: Kya customer ko app download karni padti hai?**
A: Bilkul nahi. Customer sirf QR code scan karta hai — menu browser mein khul jaata hai. AR menu bhi browser mein hi kaam karta hai. Koi app nahi.

**Q: Internet chahiye kya?**
A: Haan — platform web-based hai. Restaurant mein WiFi hona accha hai, customers ke liye aur staff ke liye dono.

**Q: ZenTable kis type ke restaurants ke liye hai?**
A: Chhote se bade sab ke liye — cafe, QSR, fine dining, food court stalls sab use kar sakte hain. Single outlet ya multiple branches — dono support karta hai.

---

## Digital Menu

**Q: Menu update karna padega to kya karna hoga?**
A: Menu mein changes ke liye ZenTable team se contact karo — woh update kar denge. Future mein owner panel se seedha edit karne ka feature aayega.

**Q: Menu mein kitne items add ho sakte hain?**
A: Koi hard limit nahi hai — jitne chahiye utne.

**Q: Menu mein images hona zaroori hai?**
A: Zaroori nahi, but strongly recommended. Images se orders 30-40% zyada hote hain.

---

## AR Menu

**Q: AR menu kaise kaam karta hai?**
A: QR code camera ke saamne rakho — 3D food model screen pe aa jaata hai. Rotate karo, zoom karo, dish explore karo — phir order karo.

**Q: 3D models kaise bante hain?**
A: ZenTable ki team banati hai — restaurant owner ko kuch nahi karna. Bas batao kaun si dishes chahiye 3D mein.

**Q: Sabhi dishes ke 3D models hona zaroori hai?**
A: Nahi — partial bhi ho sakta hai. Featured ya premium dishes ke 3D models kaafi hote hain.

**Q: AR kaun se phones pe kaam karta hai?**
A: Android (Chrome) aur iOS (Safari) — modern smartphones pe sab jagah kaam karta hai.

---

## Ordering & Operations

**Q: Customer order kaise karta hai?**
A: Table pe QR code scan karo → menu khulega → items select karo → order place karo → kitchen ko turant order milta hai.

**Q: Agar customer apna order change karna chahe?**
A: Pending order ke liye waiter se request kar sakte hain. Kitchen se clear hone ke baad changes nahi ho sakte.

**Q: Waiter ko kaise pata chalega order ready hai?**
A: Kitchen staff item ready mark karta hai → waiter ke dashboard pe show hota hai.

**Q: Kya bina table ke bhi order ho sakta hai? (takeaway)**
A: Haan — counter staff takeaway orders place kar sakta hai.

---

## Billing

**Q: Bill kaise generate hota hai?**
A: Waiter ya counter staff — "Generate Bill" button dabao. Saare done orders ka automatic bill ban jaata hai with tax aur discount options.

**Q: Kaun se payment modes support hote hain?**
A: Cash, UPI, Card — sab track hota hai. Analytics mein payment breakdown bhi dikhta hai.

**Q: Bill ka printout milega?**
A: Bill share/print option available hai.

## Staff & Access

**Q: Kitne staff accounts bana sakte hain?**
A: Plan ke hisaab se — ZenTable se confirm karo.

**Q: Staff ka password reset kaise hoga?**
A: Owner dashboard se staff ka password change kar sakte hain. Ya ZenTable support se contact karo.

**Q: Kya owner remotely dekh sakta hai kya ho raha hai restaurant mein?**
A: Haan — owner dashboard kisi bhi device se access ho sakta hai. Live orders, tables, analytics — sab real-time.

## Technical & Support

**Q: Kya ZenTable ek reliable platform hai?**
A: Platform Render pe hosted hai with PostgreSQL database aur Cloudflare CDN. Uptime monitoring active hai.

**Q: Kuch kaam na kare toh?**
A: `support@zentable.in` pe mail karo with screenshot — team working hours mein respond karti hai.

**Q: Data secure hai?**
A: Haan — JWT authentication, encrypted passwords, aur secure file storage. Customer ka data (orders, billing) sirf aapke restaurant ka staff dekh sakta hai.
