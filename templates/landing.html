How It Works

From Scan to Served in Minutes

A seamless, fully digital flow that eliminates delays and errors at every step of service.

1
📲

Customer Scans QR Code

Each table has a unique QR code. Customers scan with any smartphone — no app needed. They land on your restaurant's branded digital menu instantly.

2
📖

Browses Digital Menu

A beautifully designed digital menu loads instantly. Categories, dish photos, descriptions, prices, dietary filters, and chef recommendations — all at a glance.

3
👓

Views Dishes in AR

Customers tap any dish to see a realistic 3D AR preview on their actual table before ordering. Dramatically increases confidence and reduces order regret.

4
✅

Places the Order

Customer adds items to cart and confirms. The system logs the order instantly with table number, timestamp, quantities, and all special instructions.

5
👨‍🍳

Kitchen Receives Order Instantly

The kitchen dashboard lights up with the new order in real-time. Chefs see exactly what's needed, with priority flagging and preparation timers.

6
🍽️

Waiter Serves & Bill Generated

The waiter is notified when food is ready. They serve the table and the counter generates a complete itemized bill — ready for instant payment.
